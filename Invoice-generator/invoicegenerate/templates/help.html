Frequntly Asked Questions

Introducing Techmihirnaik Group's Network

Que: How does it work?

It's simple to send electronic invoices over the internet:
1. Sign up for a free account with Invoiced Business Network.
2. Invite your buyer to become a member of the network.
3. After connecting to your buyer on the network, you can use Invoice Generator to send invoices.

Que: How much does it cost?

Nothing! Sending and receiving invoices on Techmihirnaik group's Network is completely free.

Que: How do I get paid?

When issuing the invoice, you can choose the payment methods you want to accept.Your buyer can then choose from any of the payment options you've provided.

Que: Why it is better than email?

Your invoices will not ever get lost in someone's email inbox, or worse, the spam bin, if you use e-invoicing. Your buyer will receive line item level invoice data that they may plug into their accounts payable portal or accounting system in addition to a PDF of the invoice. As a result, your buyer will always receive your invoice and will be able to confirm payment sooner.

Que: How do I access my invoices?

Invoices are securely saved in the cloud and can be retrieved at any time for tax or legal purposes.

Que: How secure is the network?

Invoices sent through this Network are more secure than invoices sent through email.E-invoicing is also advantageous to your customers.When your buyers get bills from you via the network, they will know they are genuine.
