Sending Invoices


How do I send the invoice to my customer?
Once you’ve filled out the appropriate fields, you have two options to send the invoice to your customer:


Send through Invoice Generator
Click Send Invoice, and send the invoice using our secure email client. The invoice will be tied back to your email address, and you can use our links to PayPal, Venmo, or Square Cash for online payments. We’ve also provided standard language in the body of the email. All you have to do is review and send.


Send through your email client
Click Download Invoice. A copy of the invoice will be saved to your device. You can attach it to an email to send to your customer, or print a copy and pop it in the mail.
